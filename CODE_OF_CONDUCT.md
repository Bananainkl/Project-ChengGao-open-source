# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level,
or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while discussing ideas and code.
- Welcome questions and help newcomers participate.
- Focus criticism on the work, never on the person.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, personal attacks, sustained
disruption, sexualized content, and publishing private information without
permission are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, or other
contributions that violate this code. They may also temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when necessary.

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer privately through the contact
options on the [Bananainkl GitHub profile](https://github.com/Bananainkl).
Please do not open a public issue containing sensitive personal information.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces and whenever someone is officially
representing the project in public.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
